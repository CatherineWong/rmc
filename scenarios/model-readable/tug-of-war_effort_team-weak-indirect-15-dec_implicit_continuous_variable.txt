BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ness and Quinn beat Peyton and Jamie.
In the second match, Quinn and Max beat Peyton and Jamie.
In the third match, Quinn and Taylor beat Peyton and Jamie.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ness ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Quinn ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Peyton ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ness put into the first match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Quinn put into the first match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Peyton put into the first match?
Query 7: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Ness and Quinn will beat Max and Peyton?
Query 8: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Ness and Peyton will beat Quinn and Jamie?
