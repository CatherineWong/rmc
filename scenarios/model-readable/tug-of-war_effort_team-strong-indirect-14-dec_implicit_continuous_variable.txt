BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Sam and Emery lost to Taylor and Blake.
In the second match, Emery and Gale beat Taylor and Blake.
In the third match, Emery and Quinn beat Taylor and Blake.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Emery ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Taylor ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the first match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Emery put into the first match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Taylor put into the first match?
Query 7: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Sam and Emery will beat Gale and Taylor?
Query 8: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Sam and Taylor will beat Emery and Blake?
