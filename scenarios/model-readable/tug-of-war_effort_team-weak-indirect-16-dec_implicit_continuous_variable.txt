BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Max and Indiana lost to Peyton and Ness.
In the second match, Indiana and Val lost to Peyton and Ness.
In the third match, Indiana and Taylor lost to Peyton and Ness.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Max ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Indiana ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Peyton ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Max put into the first match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Indiana put into the first match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Peyton put into the first match?
Query 7: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Max and Indiana will beat Val and Peyton?
Query 8: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Max and Peyton will beat Indiana and Ness?
