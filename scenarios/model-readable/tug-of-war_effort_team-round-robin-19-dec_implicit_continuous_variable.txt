BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ollie and Drew beat Emery and Lane.
In the second match, Ollie and Emery beat Drew and Lane.
In the third match, Ollie and Lane beat Drew and Emery.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ollie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Drew ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lane ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ollie put into the second match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Drew put into the second match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lane put into the second match?
Query 7: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Ollie and Drew will beat Emery and Blake?
Query 8: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Ollie and Emery will beat Drew and Blake?
