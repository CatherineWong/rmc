BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Blake and Val beat Ness and Avery.
In the second match, Blake and Val lost to Ness and Avery.
In the third match, Blake and Val beat Ness and Max.
In the fourth match, Blake and Val beat Ness and Emery.
In the fifth match, Blake and Val beat Ness and Robin.

Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ness put into the second match?
