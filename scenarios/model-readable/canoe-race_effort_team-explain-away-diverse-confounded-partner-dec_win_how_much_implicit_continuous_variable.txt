BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of canoe races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Blake and Willow beat Ollie and Taylor.
In the second race, Blake and Willow beat Casey and Fey.
In the third race, Blake and Willow lost to Casey and Taylor.
In the fourth race, Blake and Willow beat Ollie and Casey.
In the fifth race, Blake and Willow beat Fey and Taylor.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Blake ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Willow ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Casey ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Blake put into the third race?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Willow put into the third race?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Casey put into the third race?
Query 7: In a new race later this same day between Blake and Willow (Team 1) and Casey and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 8: In a new race later this same day between Blake and Ollie (Team 1) and Willow and Fey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
