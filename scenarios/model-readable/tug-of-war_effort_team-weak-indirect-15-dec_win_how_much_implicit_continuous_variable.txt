BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Drew and Lane beat Casey and Robin.
In the second match, Lane and Indiana beat Casey and Robin.
In the third match, Lane and Avery beat Casey and Robin.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Drew ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lane ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Casey ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Drew put into the first match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lane put into the first match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Casey put into the first match?
Query 7: In a new match later this same day between Drew and Lane (Team 1) and Indiana and Casey (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 8: In a new match later this same day between Drew and Casey (Team 1) and Lane and Robin (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
