BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of canoe races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Gale and Drew beat Sam and Max.
In the second race, Drew and Quinn lost to Sam and Max.
In the third race, Drew and Willow lost to Sam and Max.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Gale ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Drew ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gale put into the first race?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Drew put into the first race?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the first race?
Query 7: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Gale and Drew will beat Quinn and Sam?
Query 8: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Gale and Sam will beat Drew and Max?
