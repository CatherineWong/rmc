BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Indiana and Gale beat Taylor and Ollie.
In the second match, Indiana and Gale beat Harper and Jamie.
In the third match, Indiana and Gale beat Blake and Kay.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Indiana ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Gale ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Harper ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Indiana put into the second match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gale put into the second match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Harper put into the second match?
Query 7: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Indiana and Gale will beat Taylor and Kay?
Query 8: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Indiana and Taylor will beat Gale and Ollie?
