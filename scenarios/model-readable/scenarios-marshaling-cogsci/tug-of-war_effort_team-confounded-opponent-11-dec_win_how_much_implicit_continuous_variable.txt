BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Harper and Indiana beat Ollie and Taylor.
In the second match, Harper and Gale beat Ollie and Casey.
In the third match, Harper and Robin beat Ollie and Kay.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Harper ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Indiana ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ollie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Harper put into the third match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Indiana put into the third match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ollie put into the third match?
Query 7: In a new match later this same day between Harper and Indiana (Team 1) and Gale and Kay (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 8: In a new match later this same day between Harper and Ollie (Team 1) and Indiana and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
