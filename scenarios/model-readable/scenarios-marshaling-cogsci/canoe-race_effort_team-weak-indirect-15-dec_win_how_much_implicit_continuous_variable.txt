BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of canoe races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Fey and Ness beat Val and Ollie.
In the second race, Ness and Gale beat Val and Ollie.
In the third race, Ness and Robin beat Val and Ollie.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Fey ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ness ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Val ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Fey put into the first race?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ness put into the first race?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Val put into the first race?
Query 7: In a new race later this same day between Fey and Ness (Team 1) and Gale and Val (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 8: In a new race later this same day between Fey and Val (Team 1) and Ness and Ollie (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
