BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Val and Sam lost to Robin and Indiana.
In the second match, Val and Robin lost to Sam and Indiana.
In the third match, Val and Indiana lost to Sam and Robin.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Val ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Robin ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Val put into the second match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the second match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Robin put into the second match?
Query 7: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Val and Sam will beat Robin and Quinn?
Query 8: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Val and Robin will beat Sam and Quinn?
