BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of canoe races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Fey and Taylor lost to Indiana and Robin.
In the second race, Fey and Taylor lost to Harper and Ness.
In the third race, Fey and Taylor lost to Willow and Peyton.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Fey ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Taylor ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Willow ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Fey put into the third race?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Taylor put into the third race?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Willow put into the third race?
Query 7: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Fey and Taylor will beat Indiana and Peyton?
Query 8: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Fey and Indiana will beat Taylor and Robin?
