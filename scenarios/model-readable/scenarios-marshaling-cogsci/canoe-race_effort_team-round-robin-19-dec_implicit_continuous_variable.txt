BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of canoe races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Emery and Ness beat Willow and Gale.
In the second race, Emery and Willow beat Ness and Gale.
In the third race, Emery and Gale beat Ness and Willow.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Emery ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ness ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Gale ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Emery put into the second race?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ness put into the second race?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gale put into the second race?
Query 7: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Emery and Ness will beat Willow and Lane?
Query 8: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Emery and Willow will beat Ness and Lane?
