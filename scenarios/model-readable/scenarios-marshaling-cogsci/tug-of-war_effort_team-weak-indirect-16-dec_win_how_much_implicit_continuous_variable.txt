BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Emery and Peyton lost to Blake and Gale.
In the second match, Peyton and Lane lost to Blake and Gale.
In the third match, Peyton and Avery lost to Blake and Gale.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Emery ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Peyton ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Blake ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Emery put into the first match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Peyton put into the first match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Blake put into the first match?
Query 7: In a new match later this same day between Emery and Peyton (Team 1) and Lane and Blake (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 8: In a new match later this same day between Emery and Blake (Team 1) and Peyton and Gale (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
