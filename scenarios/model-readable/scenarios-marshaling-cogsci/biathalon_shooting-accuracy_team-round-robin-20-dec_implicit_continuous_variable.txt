BACKGROUND
In this event, teams of players are competing in rounds of a biathalon, a winter sport that combines cross-country skiing races and rifle shooting. In each round, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to ski, based on their intrinsic strength, as well as each team member's shooting accuracy in that particular round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first round, Robin and Blake lost to Drew and Max.
In the second round, Robin and Drew lost to Blake and Max.
In the third round, Robin and Max lost to Blake and Drew.

QUERIES

Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Blake ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Drew ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how accurate do you think Robin was at shooting in the second round?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how accurate do you think Blake was at shooting in the second round?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how accurate do you think Drew was at shooting in the second round?
