BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Willow and Casey beat Emery and Taylor.
In the second match, Willow and Fey beat Avery and Quinn.
In the third match, Willow and Indiana beat Ollie and Gale.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Willow ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Casey ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Emery ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Willow put into the second match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Casey put into the first match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Emery put into the first match?
Query 7: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Willow and Casey will beat Emery and Quinn?
Query 8: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Willow and Emery will beat Casey and Taylor?
