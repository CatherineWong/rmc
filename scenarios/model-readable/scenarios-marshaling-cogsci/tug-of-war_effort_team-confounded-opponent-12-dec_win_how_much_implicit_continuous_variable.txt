BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Robin and Ness lost to Gale and Blake.
In the second match, Robin and Jamie lost to Gale and Sam.
In the third match, Robin and Avery lost to Gale and Kay.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Robin ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ness ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Gale ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Robin put into the second match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ness put into the first match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Gale put into the second match?
Query 7: In a new match later this same day between Robin and Ness (Team 1) and Jamie and Kay (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 8: In a new match later this same day between Robin and Gale (Team 1) and Ness and Blake (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
