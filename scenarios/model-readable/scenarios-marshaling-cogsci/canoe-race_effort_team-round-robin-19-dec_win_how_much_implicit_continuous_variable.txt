BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of canoe races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Avery and Kay beat Fey and Emery.
In the second race, Avery and Fey beat Kay and Emery.
In the third race, Avery and Emery beat Kay and Fey.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Avery ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Kay ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Emery ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Avery put into the second race?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Kay put into the second race?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Emery put into the second race?
Query 7: In a new race later this same day between Avery and Kay (Team 1) and Fey and Indiana (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 8: In a new race later this same day between Avery and Fey (Team 1) and Kay and Indiana (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
