BACKGROUND
In this event, teams of players are competing in rounds of a biathalon, a winter sport that combines cross-country skiing and rifle shooting. Teams compete in relays. Each team member both skis and shoots.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.
