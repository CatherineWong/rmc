BACKGROUND
In this event, teams of players are competing in rounds of a synchronized diving tournament. In synchronized diving, team members are jumping simultaneously and scores depend both on overall execution and how well matched the team members are.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Indiana and Ness beat Jamie and Casey.
In the second match, Ness and Ollie beat Jamie and Casey.
In the third match, Ness and Val beat Jamie and Casey.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Indiana ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ness ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic skill?
Query 4: How much effort do you think Indiana put into the first match?
Query 5: How much effort do you think Ness put into the first match?
Query 6: How much effort do you think Jamie put into the first match?
Query 7: In a new match, how likely is it that Indiana and Ness will beat Ollie and Jamie?
Query 8: In a new match, how likely is it that Indiana and Jamie will beat Ness and Casey?
