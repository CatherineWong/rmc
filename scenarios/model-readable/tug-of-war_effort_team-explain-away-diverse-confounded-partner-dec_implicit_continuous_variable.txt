BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Ness and Indiana beat Drew and Robin.
In the second match, Ness and Indiana beat Taylor and Jamie.
In the third match, Ness and Indiana lost to Taylor and Robin.
In the fourth match, Ness and Indiana beat Drew and Taylor.
In the fifth match, Ness and Indiana beat Jamie and Robin.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ness ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Indiana ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Taylor ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ness put into the third match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Indiana put into the third match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Taylor put into the third match?
Query 7: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Ness and Indiana will beat Taylor and Robin?
Query 8: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Ness and Drew will beat Indiana and Jamie?
