BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Lane and Sam beat Max and Taylor.
In the second match, Sam and Gale lost to Max and Taylor.
In the third match, Sam and Jamie lost to Max and Taylor.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lane ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Max ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lane put into the first match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the first match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Max put into the first match?
Query 7: In a new match later this same day between Lane and Sam (Team 1) and Gale and Max (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 8: In a new match later this same day between Lane and Max (Team 1) and Sam and Taylor (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
