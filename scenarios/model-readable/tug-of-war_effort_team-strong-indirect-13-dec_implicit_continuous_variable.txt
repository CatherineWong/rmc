BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in matches of tug-of-war. In each round, the team that wins the round depends on how hard the athletes collectively pull, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first match, Fey and Ollie beat Val and Quinn.
In the second match, Ollie and Ness lost to Val and Quinn.
In the third match, Ollie and Gale lost to Val and Quinn.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Fey ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ollie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Val ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Fey put into the first match?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ollie put into the first match?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Val put into the first match?
Query 7: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Fey and Ollie will beat Ness and Val?
Query 8: In a new match later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Fey and Val will beat Ollie and Quinn?
