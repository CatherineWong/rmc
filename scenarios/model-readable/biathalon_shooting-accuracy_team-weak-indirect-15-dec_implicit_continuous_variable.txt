BACKGROUND
In this event, teams of players are competing in rounds of a biathalon, a winter sport that combines cross-country skiing races and rifle shooting. In each round, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to ski, based on their intrinsic strength, as well as each team member's shooting accuracy in that particular round.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first round, Harper and Jamie beat Drew and Casey.
In the second round, Jamie and Gale beat Drew and Casey.
In the third round, Jamie and Robin beat Drew and Casey.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Harper ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Drew ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how accurate do you think Harper was at shooting in the first round?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how accurate do you think Jamie was at shooting in the first round?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how accurate do you think Drew was at shooting in the first round?
Query 7: In a new round later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Harper and Jamie will beat Gale and Drew?
Query 8: In a new round later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Harper and Drew will beat Jamie and Casey?
