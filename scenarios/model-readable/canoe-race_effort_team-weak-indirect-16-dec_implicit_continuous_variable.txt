BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of canoe races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Willow and Robin lost to Harper and Ness.
In the second race, Robin and Sam lost to Harper and Ness.
In the third race, Robin and Peyton lost to Harper and Ness.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Willow ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Robin ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Harper ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Willow put into the first race?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Robin put into the first race?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Harper put into the first race?
Query 7: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Willow and Robin will beat Sam and Harper?
Query 8: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Willow and Harper will beat Robin and Ness?
