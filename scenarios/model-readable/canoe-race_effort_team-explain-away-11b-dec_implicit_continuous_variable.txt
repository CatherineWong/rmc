BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of canoe races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Taylor and Jamie lost to Robin and Indiana.
In the second race, Taylor and Peyton lost to Robin and Blake.
In the third race, Taylor and Emery lost to Robin and Casey.
In the fourth race, Taylor and Jamie beat Robin and Indiana.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Taylor ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Robin ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Taylor put into the fourth race?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jamie put into the fourth race?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Robin put into the fourth race?
Query 7: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Taylor and Jamie will beat Peyton and Casey?
Query 8: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Taylor and Robin will beat Jamie and Indiana?
