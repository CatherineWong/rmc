BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of canoe races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Fey and Ollie lost to Lane and Jamie.
In the second race, Fey and Harper lost to Lane and Emery.
In the third race, Fey and Blake lost to Lane and Gale.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Fey ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Ollie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Lane ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Fey put into the second race?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Ollie put into the first race?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Lane put into the second race?
Query 7: In a new race later this same day between Fey and Ollie (Team 1) and Harper and Gale (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
Query 8: In a new race later this same day between Fey and Lane (Team 1) and Ollie and Jamie (Team 2), who would win and by how much?
