BACKGROUND
In this event, the athletes are competing in a series of canoe races. In each race, the team that wins depends on the average speed with which the athletes are able to row, based on their intrinsic strength modulated by other factors including how much effort they put in to that race.
Athletes compete either individually or as a team.
All matches take place on the same day.

CONDITIONS
In the first race, Harper and Sam beat Jamie and Avery.
In the second race, Harper and Val beat Willow and Emery.
In the third race, Harper and Casey beat Ness and Quinn.

QUERIES
Query 1: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Harper ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 2: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Sam ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 3: Out of 100 random athletes, where do you think Jamie ranks in terms of intrinsic strength?
Query 4: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Harper put into the second race?
Query 5: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Sam put into the first race?
Query 6: On a percentage scale from 0 to 100%, how much effort do you think Jamie put into the first race?
Query 7: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Harper and Sam will beat Jamie and Emery?
Query 8: In a new race later today during this same tournament, how likely is it that Harper and Jamie will beat Sam and Avery?
